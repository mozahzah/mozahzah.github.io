About

As an audio developer, my work focuses on bridging the gap between technology and creativity. I enjoy building tools for audio implementers, working with composers, and partnering with sound designers.

Currently, I'm part of the team at Archetype Entertainment, working on the upcoming game Exodus (exodusgame.com). My role involves solving a variety of engineering challenges, ranging from high-level system design to low-level optimizations.

In my free time, you’ll often find me diving into audio DSP, contributing to open-source projects, or tinkering with anything that makes sound.

Always learning. Always listening.
